## ✨ Features

- 🎣 **Auto Fishing**
  - 🐟 Auto-sell fish
  - 🪝 Auto-buy bait
- 🥤 **Auto Coffee Making**
  - 🔨 Evict all customers
- 💰 **Auto Collect Café Revenue**
  - 📦 Auto-restock items
- 💎 **Auto Claim Rewards**
  - 🎁 Daily Activity
  - 📅 Battle Pass (Period Rewards)
- 🕛 **Real-time Assistance**
  - 🗼 Auto-teleport
  - ⏩ Auto-skip story/dialogue
- ⚔️ **Auto Dodge**
  - 🛡️ Audio recognition-based Auto Dodge/Counter
- 🎵 **Auto Music Performance**
  - 🥁 Loop playing "Meisei Kyō" (迷星叫)
